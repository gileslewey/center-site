Enterprise Leadership

Enterprise Leadership Program—A custom program for top 100 partners of a Professional Services Firm that caters to the middle-market. Participants were immersed in a 16-month collaborative journey stretching their capacity to hold the context, perspective and resilience required to think on behalf of the enterprise, while reconciling the tensions that emerge from needing to take care of business locally at the same time. The Firm’s senior leadership team partnered with CCBL as faculty for the program creating an opportunity for next generation leaders to engage at an entirely new level with their senior leadership team.

The program has become a key fixture in the firm’s overall leadership curriculum affording them with substantially increased partnership across boundaries and key successors for senior leadership roles.

Support for Emerging Leaders

Emerging Leader Program—This program is custom-made for emerging leaders. It focuses on managers with 5-8 years of experience. With the current trends around “The Great Resignation” developing this group of leaders is especially important. If these leaders aren’t fully equipped for their leadership role – they become less effective in developing and retaining the seniors and associates who work for them. When those team members leave, managers become overwhelmed with work – further impacting their leadership effectiveness and stress levels. This program runs over a 12-month period and demonstrates a commitment to growing leaders at the manager level. It’s geared towards helping participants make positive changes, both personally and professionally, with a cascading impact on those who work on their engagements. It focuses on knowing your leadership impact, fostering trust and community in teams, and delivering results that matter.

Growth Academy

Growth Academy—A custom program created for a Member Association Firm focused on Accounting and Consulting. The program helps sr managers and new partners enhance their leadership effectiveness while strengthening relationships across the network of member firms. It runs over an 18-month period—strategically-paced around peak business cycles. The curriculum strengthens the capacity of these leaders to think broader and:

  • Strengthen executive presence
  • Shift from self-reliance to team reliance, coach and develop talent
  • Step up to difficult conversations
  • Deliver the results that matter most
  • Create the future by inspiring and engaging others

The Growth Academy consists of 4 in-person (or virtual) sessions, 1-1 coaching session with a CCBL coach in between sessions, and peer group coaching. Peer groups meet as part of the live session as well as a total of 6 times (self-directed) during the 18-month program. The program is on its 5th Cohort and has over doubled in size.
